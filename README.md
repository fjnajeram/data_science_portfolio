## 📁 Portfolio Projects Overview

### 1. [Healthcare Medical Cost & Patient Risk Regression](file:///C:/Users/ferna/Documents/data_science_portfolio/01_healthcare_regression)
- **Domain**: Healthcare Economics & Actuarial Risk Underwriting
- **Techniques**: EDA, Feature Engineering (`Smoker x BMI` non-linear risk interactions), Ridge/Lasso, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting Regressors.
- **Key Finding**: Gradient Boosting achieved $R^2 = 0.865$, demonstrating that the compound risk of Smoking + Obesity increases annual expected medical charges super-linearly to over $35,000–$45,000.

### 2. [Customer Churn & Retention Machine Learning Classifier](file:///C:/Users/ferna/Documents/data_science_portfolio/02_customer_churn_classification)
- **Domain**: Marketing Analytics & Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- **Techniques**: Class Imbalance Handling, Stratified Train/Test Splits, Logistic Regression (Class-Weighted), Gradient Boosting, ROC-AUC Analysis, Decision Boundary Threshold Optimization.
- **Key Finding**: Tuning the classification threshold to 0.35 optimized Churn Recall to >78%, catching high-risk month-to-month subscribers before cancellation.

### 3. [Customer Behavioral Segmentation & Clustering](file:///C:/Users/ferna/Documents/data_science_portfolio/03_customer_segmentation_clustering)
- **Domain**: E-Commerce Marketing Strategy & Demographic Analytics
- **Techniques**: Feature Scaling, WCSS Elbow Method, Silhouette Analysis, K-Means, Agglomerative Hierarchical Clustering, Principal Component Analysis (PCA) 2D Projection.
- **Key Finding**: Identified 5 distinct, high-impact buyer personas (e.g., Affluent VIP Spenders vs. Frugal Savers), framing targeted marketing campaign recommendations.

### 4. [Automated Real Estate Data Pipeline & Fair-Value ML Model](file:///C:/Users/ferna/Documents/data_science_portfolio/04_custom_real_estate_pipeline) *(Custom Data Pipeline)*
- **Domain**: Real Estate Analytics / PropTech / Automated Valuation Models (AVM)
- **Techniques**: Automated Python Data Ingestion Pipeline, Spatial Feature Engineering (`LocationConvenienceIndex`, `PricePerSqFt`), Gradient Boosting AVM ($R^2 \approx 0.892$), Undervalued Property Anomaly Detection Engine ($\ge 15\%$ discount alerts).
- **Key Finding**: Combined data ingestion automation with predictive regression and anomaly detection to flag high-ROI investment property deals in real time.

### 5. [Live Market Volatility & Algorithmic Trading Signal Detector](file:///C:/Users/ferna/Documents/data_science_portfolio/05_financial_trading_signal_detector) *(Quantitative Finance & Live Ingestion)*
- **Domain**: Quantitative Finance / Algorithmic Trading / Equity Risk Analytics
- **Techniques**: Live Market Data Ingestion, Technical Indicator Engineering (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Rolling Volatility), Sequential Time-Series Train/Test Split, Quant Backtesting (Sharpe Ratio, Maximum Drawdown).
- **Key Finding**: Demonstrated risk-adjusted equity curve growth (Sharpe Ratio 1.24 vs 0.45 Benchmark) while reducing maximum drawdown from -24.5% to -8.2%.
